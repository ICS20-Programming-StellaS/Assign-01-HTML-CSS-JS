How You Can Help

Show Support

By choosing to help, donate and support this crisis and the thousands of people around the world who need your help, you are changing and making a difference in someone's life.

There are so many ways you can help and here's how:

1. Donate to organizations 2. Donate clothing, used goods or anything to refugee families in need. 3. Spread awareness, expand your knowledge and educate yourself about this crisis.

These are all things that you can do that don't require much. Plus they are all things that will help make someone's life better. Even just educating yourself. By knowing this topic and by knowing something, can show that you're educated on this subject and are aware of what's going on. It shows that you haven't forgotten.
